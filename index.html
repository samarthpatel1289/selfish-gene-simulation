Hawk vs Dove Simulation

🦅 Hawk — always fights aggressively for a resource. If it meets another Hawk, they battle until one is injured.

🕊️ Dove — displays peacefully and retreats rather than risk injury. Against a Hawk it always backs down; against another Dove they share.

Bourgeois vs Intruder Simulation

🏠 Bourgeois — plays "owner wins": fights like a Hawk when at home, retreats like a Dove when intruding elsewhere.

⚔️ Intruder — always attacks, regardless of ownership. Equivalent to a pure Hawk that ignores ownership conventions.

💡 Bourgeois wins because both sides agree on the ownership rule — no costly injuries ever happen!
